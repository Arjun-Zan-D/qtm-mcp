## 1. Research and Educational Use Only
This repository, including its source code, CLI commands, model configurations, and tool integrations (collectively, the "Software"), is provided strictly for **research, laboratory, and educational purposes**. 

It is designed to serve as a bridge between AI models (such as LLMs) and the Qualisys Track Manager (QTM) real-time or offline biomechanical data. It is **not** designed, intended, or tested for:
* Diagnosing, treating, curing, or preventing any disease, pathological gait pattern, musculoskeletal disorder, or other medical condition.
* Providing primary diagnostic data for orthopedic surgical planning, physical therapy prescribing, or rehabilitation tracking.
* Serving as an active patient monitoring system.

## 2. AI-Generated Insights and Data Streams
This Software exposes raw kinematic, analog (EMG), and force plate streams to Large Language Models (LLMs) and other AI agents to assist with data organization and automation. 

**You explicitly acknowledge and agree that:**
* AI-generated summaries, biomechanical clinical reports, and postural evaluations are **predictive, non-diagnostic, and subject to error (hallucination or calculation inaccuracies)**.
* Spatio-temporal gait parameters (e.g., stance time, swing time, stride length) and kinematic joint angles calculated by secondary pipelines (such as MATLAB or OpenSim wrappers) must be verified programmatically and clinically.
* No patient care decisions, surgical interventions, orthotic designs, or pharmaceutical adjustments should be based directly on outputs from the Software.
